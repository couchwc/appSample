The Granville Inn

A staple of Old Louisville and a favorite of the students at UofL.

Bar/Kitchen Hours

The bar opens in the morning at eleven Monday through Saturday and then on Sunday at one in the afternoon. The kitchen is open everyday from the time we open until midnight. The bar stays open until four in the morning every night.

Happy Hour

Join us during happy hour! Monday through Friday starting at three in the afternoon.

Gameday Specials

C-A-R-D-S CARDS!

Come in and cheer on the Cards! Football, Basketball, Baseball! If the Cards are on, we will be showing them! And do not forget about our drink specials!

Are You Ready For Some Football!

The Granville is the place to watch college football on Saturday and the NFL on Sunday and Monday! Again do not forget to ask about our specials!

Trivia!

Trivia is held on Tuesday evenings and starts at seven. Come outsmart the competition while enjoying our drink specials and dollar slices of pizza!
